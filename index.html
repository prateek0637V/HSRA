Who We Are

The Hindustan Socialist Republican Association (HSRA) stands as a testament to the undying spirit of freedom, unity, and social equality. Founded with a vision to liberate the masses from oppression, we continue to uphold the values of our forefathers—dedication to the collective good, unwavering discipline, and the pursuit of a just society.


We are a union of heritage, committed to educating the present generation about the sacrifices of the past while building a stronger, more equitable future for India.

Our Objectives

Unity & Responsibility

To promote a sense of unity and collective responsibility among all members of society, transcending caste and creed.

Social Equality

To encourage social and economic equality, ensuring that the fruits of development reach the last person in the line.

Preserving Heritage

To preserve and propagate the revolutionary heritage and values of the HSRA, inspiring new generations.

Ethical Participation

To inspire disciplined, ethical, and active participation in nation-building activities.
